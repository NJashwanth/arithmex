Free, fast, and accurate calculators — built for everyone.

Our Mission

Arithmex was built with one goal: give everyone access to powerful, reliable calculators without paywalls, sign-ups, or confusing interfaces. Whether you're calculating a mortgage, checking your BMI, converting units, or counting down to an event — Arithmex has you covered.

Every tool on Arithmex runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, no account is required, and the service is completely free.
