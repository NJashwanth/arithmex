How Arithmex Works

Simple, powerful, and completely private.

Arithmex is a collection of free online calculators designed to help students, professionals, and everyday users solve real-world problems quickly and accurately. Every calculator runs entirely in your web browser using JavaScript — no data is ever sent to a server, and no account or sign-up is required.

Whether you need to calculate a monthly mortgage payment, check your BMI, convert kilometers to miles, find your age in days, or estimate your daily calorie needs, Arithmex provides instant, reliable results. All calculations use established scientific and financial formulas, including the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for metabolic rate, Naegele's Rule for pregnancy due dates, standard amortization formulas for loans, and IEEE 754 floating-point arithmetic for scientific calculations.

What You Can Calculate

  • Universal Calculator: Scientific calculations with trigonometry, logarithms, exponents, factorials, and full calculation history saved locally.
  • Finance Calculator: Loan amortization schedules, compound interest projections, savings goals, ROI/CAGR analysis, and currency conversion.
  • Fitness Calculator: Body Mass Index (BMI), Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), macronutrient ratios, heart rate training zones, and one-rep max estimates.
  • Health Calculator: Daily water intake, Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), body fat percentage via US Navy method, pregnancy due date, and ovulation window.
  • Time Calculator: Exact age calculation, duration between dates, timezone conversion with world clock, work hours with overtime pay, and countdown timers.
  • Unit Converter: Conversion across 7 categories — length, weight, volume, area, speed, temperature, and digital storage — covering both metric and imperial systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Arithmex really free?

Yes, completely. Every calculator on Arithmex is free to use with no limits, no sign-ups, and no paywalls. The site is supported by non-intrusive advertising.

Is my data safe?

All calculations happen locally in your browser. Your inputs and results are never transmitted to any server. Calculator history and theme preferences are stored in your browser's local storage, which only you can access. You can clear this data at any time.

Can I use Arithmex on my phone?

Yes. Arithmex is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. The interface adapts to your screen size for a comfortable experience on any device.

How accurate are the calculators?

Our calculators use established, peer-reviewed formulas and JavaScript's IEEE 754 math engine. Results are accurate to approximately 15 significant digits. However, calculators are for informational purposes — always consult a qualified professional for medical, financial, or legal decisions.
